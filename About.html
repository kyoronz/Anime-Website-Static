About us

Thank you for reading and paying attention on Four Leaves. Four Leaves is fan based anime news portal, which is created by our wonderful team member.

Why we create this “Four Leaves”?

As we cannot find a website that have the complete news information about anime, manga, light novel, visual novel, music album. Hence, we created a website that can hold all the information in one stop.

What are the features of our website?

Our website have chatroom which can be used for chit-chatting. We also have the forum for discussion related to anime. We also have calendar to allow reader to keep track on the event date and time.

If you have any latest news related to the anime or manga, feels free to email us to make our website the best news portal for you and everyone.

Email: fourleaves@hotmail.com

Hope you enjoy it.
